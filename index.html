Get to know the Maps

Factory

Factory is the first game map ever created in Delta Bourne, its also the oldest.   The Factory map was created in Feburary and and the last update for the map was in early March.


Aircraft Carrier

This is the best map ive ever created in terms of me honoring my uncle who was in the navy.   It was created in Feburary through March.


Vietnam

Vietnam is a map that i created to take the place of a military base.   This map was created in late Feburary and was updated in March


Mini Ctf

Mini Ctf is a map that was made for the ctf game mode.   It is a golf course with glass walls around it.   This map was made in March.


Airport

The airport map is one of my least favorite maps created,   The reason why is because the planes dont have textures because i wanted to optimize the map as much as i could.   This map was create in March.
